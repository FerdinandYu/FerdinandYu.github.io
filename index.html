Hey, how's your day going? Thanks for visiting and welcome to my portofolio site! Bienvenue et bonne chance pour découvrir quelque chose d'intéressant!

I'm currently pursuing a Masters in Data Science for Business at École Polytechnique and HEC Paris in France and will graduate in June 2021 (hopefully). I held a Bachelor's degree in Labor Economics from Renmin University of China and did a semester exchange at Cornell University (in Economics as well). My interest in Data Science was greatly inspired by Econometrics with which I was able to test many ideas that had once haunted my mind with real-world data. BUT, let me be honest, switching from Economics to Data Science is not that easy, not only because of the lack of exposure to Mathematics and Computer Science, but also due to the different approaches to problems (e.g. more hypothesis-driven versus more data-driven). On the other side, however, I found my training in humanities and social sciences extremely helpful in collecting proper model features and in interpreting results. Now I'm thriving to master both sides! (wish me good luck) I would like to share my ideas and approaches with this portofilio site. Hope you will like it!

UPDATE: I'll be doing an internship at ByteDance as a commercialization Product Manager intern until September. My responsibilities mainly include optimizing bidding strategy and precision delivery with machine learning and data mining techniques for the ads on ByteDance's overseas products like TikTok, Musical.ly, etc. This domain in general is called computational advertising and I feel really excited about it. I would be glad to share with you some of my insights gained from this internship.

(p.s. Don't hesitate to leave some comments! I would greatly appreciate them!)
